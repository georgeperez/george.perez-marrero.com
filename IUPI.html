RRPADM on macOS Sierra and up (including High Sierra)

Instructions

Luckily for UPRRP students, their infrastructure is more modern than the one in UPRM. Thus, there's no need for overly complicated instructions. I strongly recommend not using telnet like it says on your web portal and instead use SSH. Just enter the following command in Terminal:

ssh matricula@rrpadm.uprrp.edu

Press Return.

When you're asked for a password, rather than using the password provided by the MIUPI portal, use enero2018. You should now be connected to the RRPADM system.

Please send any questions or feedback to my email.
